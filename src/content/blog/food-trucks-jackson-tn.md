Jackson has quietly become one of the best cities in West Tennessee for street food. You just have to know where to look.

That's the whole reason we built this site. Food trucks move. They park at a brewery one weekend and a church lot the next. By the time word gets around, the truck is gone. So we put every truck we can find on one [free map](/map/), and we keep it updated as new trucks roll in.

Here's what makes Jackson special. The trucks here cook like they're feeding family. You'll find smoked brisket that falls apart on the fork. Street tacos with handmade tortillas. Burgers stacked tall enough to need both hands. And the owners remember your name by the second visit.

Most trucks set up near downtown, along the Highway 45 Bypass, or at local events like food truck Fridays. Lunch hours are your best bet. The popular trucks sell out, and they sell out fast.

We update this guide as new trucks join the map. Every truck in Jackson gets listed for free. No fees and no favorites. If you own a truck and you're not on the map yet, [add yourself here](/map/#register). It takes about two minutes.

Hungry now? Open the [map](/map/) and see who's parked near you today. Then go say hi. Tell them WestTNEats sent you.
